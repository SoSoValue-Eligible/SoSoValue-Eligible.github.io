When you hold, stake, or lock SoSoValue Indexes(SSI) tokens, staking $SOSO tokens can increase your SSI mining rewards by 10% to 100%. The amount of the boost depends on how much $SOSO you stake relative to the total value of your SSI assets.
If you transfer sSOSO(Staking certificate obtained after staking $SOSO) to another wallet, you will no longer receive the boost rewards.

Example:

If you stake $100,000 in MAG7.ssi,
to increase your rewards by 10%, you need to stake $10,000 in $SOSO.
to increase your rewards by 100%, you need to stake $100,000 in $SOSO.

Minimum Boost Requirement:

To activate the 10% boost, you must stake at least 10% of your SSI assets’ value in $SOSO. If you stake less than 10%, the boost will not apply.

1. A random daily snapshot of token holdings and staked amounts is taken within ±3 hours of 12:00 PM UTC.
2. Points Formula:
Weighted Portfolio = (Token Holdings × Holding Multiplier) + (Staked Tokens × Staking Multiplier).
Daily Points = (User’s Weighted Portfolio ÷ Total Weighted Holdings) × 100,000,000.

Example:

3. User A holds 100 MAG7.ssi tokens (Holding Multiplier = 3) and stakes 100 MAG7.ssi tokens (Staking Multiplier = 15).
Weighted Portfolio = (100 × 3) + (100 × 15) = 1,800.
4. If total weighted holdings for the day are 1,800,000, User A’s share = 1,800 ÷ 1,800,000 = 0.1%.
5. Daily Points = 100,000,000 × 0.1% = 100,000 points.

Snapshots are taken around 12:00 PM UTC, within a ±3-hour window.

After unstaking your SSI tokens, there is a 14-day cooldown period before you can withdraw them to your wallet.
  • If you unstake before 8:00 PM UTC on February 1, 2025, you won't earn points during the cooldown period.
  • If you unstake after 8:00 PM UTC on February 1, 2025, you will continue earning staked points (15X) on your SSI during the cooldown period.
